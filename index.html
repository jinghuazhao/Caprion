Coding

  • Pilot
    • caprion.R and caprion.ini are for data processing. Their derivatives are in the utils/ subdirectory:
      • utils/affymetrix.sh is for variant-specific association analysis.
      • utils/qctool.sb is used to extract available sample and genotypes.
      • utils/qctool.sh further extracts genotypes with MAF 0.01 only.
      • utils/plink2.sh non-SLURM version of association analysis.
      • utils/qqman.sh and utils/qqman.R produce QQ and Manhattan plots.
      • utils/sentinels_nold.sh and utils/merge.sh select sentinels.
      • utils/ps.sh and utils/ps.R run through PhenoScanner.
      • utils/lookup.sh looks up for overlap with SomaLogic and Olink.
      • caprion.ipynb is a Jupyter notebook with some preprocessing done by utils/tensorqtl.sh.
